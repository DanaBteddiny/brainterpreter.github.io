Circuit & PCB Layout

The Brainterpreter project incorporates custom-designed PCBs (Printed Circuit Boards) and optimized circuit layouts to ensure robust and reliable communication between components. These designs are carefully crafted to minimize signal interference, reduce latency, and provide stable power distribution to all connected devices.

The custom PCBs are used in the smart plugs to house the ESP32 microcontrollers, relay modules, and power regulation circuits. This enables safe and efficient control of high-voltage appliances directly through brainwave commands. Each PCB is designed with careful consideration of current flow, heat dissipation, and signal integrity, ensuring durability and reliability in real-world usage.
