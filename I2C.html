Inter-Integrated Circuit

The Inter-Integrated Circuit (I2C) communication protocol is a widely used standard that enables efficient and reliable data exchange between multiple devices over just two wires: SDA (Serial Data) and SCL (Serial Clock). Designed for simplicity and scalability, I2C allows microcontrollers like the Raspberry Pi to communicate directly with peripheral devices such as the ESP32, sensors, and displays with minimal wiring.

In Brainterpreter, I2C serves as the backbone for data transmission between the Raspberry Pi and the ESP32 sender module, ensuring that mental commands processed by the Emotiv Insight are sent instantly and securely to the processing unit, Raspberry Pi. This low-latency connection allows for real-time response, a critical requirement for hands-free smart home control.
